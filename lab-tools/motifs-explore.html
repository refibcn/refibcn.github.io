Symbiotic Organism

Role: A composite motif — it brings Neural Networks (relational structure), Flows (movement and rhythm), and Tissues (organic, layered surface) together as one symbiotic whole.

Think “single living visual” that reads as network + motion + soft organic matter at once, not three separate decorations.

Tissues

Role: Organic, low-contrast texture and layering — soft surfaces, membranes, depth without hard geometry.

Supportive background language; pairs with the network and flow reads inside the symbiotic composite.

Polygon mesh: Voronoi cells filled as lumens; walls stroked on mesh edges. Hotspots seed macro lumens + growth; patch extent = BFS on the cell graph (no hull mask). Outer boundary = exposed facets only. Click add hotspot · drag move · Shift+click remove. Colors from Colors.

Neural Networks

Role: Relational structure — nodes, edges, graphs, “who connects to whom” at a glance.

Illustration and data-viz language for maps and dashboards; one ingredient inside the symbiotic composite.

Flows

Role: Movement and rhythm — direction, transition, breath between regions; implied or animated motion over time.

Section-to-section energy, not a static fill; another ingredient inside the symbiotic composite.
