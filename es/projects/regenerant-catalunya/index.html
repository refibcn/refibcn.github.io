Regenerant Catalunya

11 projects · 2 networks · €11,000 matching pool · funded through Gitcoin Grants Round 24.

Regenerant Catalunya unites a diverse cohort of regenerative initiatives across Catalonia under one funding umbrella. Projects were scouted by two trusted local networks — Miceli Social (rural) and La Fundició / Keras Buti (urban) — and back a broad definition of regeneration that spans ecological restoration, community projects, and civic innovation.

↗ Regenerant Catalunya site

Rural network

Miceli Social

A cooperative service hub within the social economy focused on rural resilience, based in Ripoll (Girona). Catalyzes regenerative development in rural areas by supporting municipalities and community groups.

€6,000 · 6 projects

Urban network

La Fundició / Keras Buti

A cultural and social innovation cooperative and official hub within the Xarxa d'Ateneus Cooperatius, based in L'Hospitalet de Llobregat. Brings a strong urban and peri-urban lens to the cohort.
