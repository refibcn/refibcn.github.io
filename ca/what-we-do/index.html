How an engagement runs

  1. 01

    Intro call

    Free and short. Tell us what you're working on — a network, a territory, a program — and we'll say honestly whether we can help.

  2. 02

    Short proposal

    A few days later you get options and a price, in plain language. No obligation either way.

  3. 03

    Build together

    We work in tools your team owns — living databases, maps, and workflows, not consultant decks that go stale.

  4. 04

    Handover

    You keep the systems and the practice to maintain them. Cooperative governance stays in charge; the tooling serves it.
