Who we serve

Who we work with

We work with organizations that are already doing regenerative or cooperative work and need the systems, funding pathways, or facilitation to do more of it. Primarily across Catalonia — where the Economia Social i Solidària tradition runs deep — and secondarily with Italian and Brazilian organizations we share language and culture with.

Network coordination Regenerative finance

Cooperatives & ESS organizations

Cooperatives, associations, and social-economy enterprises sitting on knowledge, processes, and relationships they can't yet see clearly. We structure that into living systems, automate the repetitive parts, and — where it helps — open transparent funding pathways through tools like Safe and Gitcoin. Cooperative governance stays in charge; the tooling serves it.

You're a fit if…

  • Your knowledge lives in people's heads and scattered drives
  • Repetitive admin eats the time that should go to the mission
  • Funding pathways feel opaque to your members
Network coordination Collective intelligence

Regenerative networks & programs

Place-based networks running programs across many partners and territories. We design and facilitate the program, map the portfolio into something navigable, and coordinate multi-stakeholder funding rounds — as we did for Regenerant Catalunya.

You're a fit if…

  • Many partners, many territories, no shared picture of the whole
  • A funding round to run across stakeholders who don't share tools
  • Coordination sits on one person's memory
Collective intelligence

Community land trusts, time banks & commons projects

Commons-oriented projects that need their shared resources made legible and durable. We build knowledge commons that stay maintainable and grow without collapsing into clutter, so a community can actually use what it holds.

You're a fit if…

  • Shared resources scattered across private drives and chat threads
  • Documentation that decays faster than it grows
  • New members can't find what the community already knows
Regenerative finance Network coordination

Funders & ReFi infrastructure

Funders and global regenerative-finance infrastructure looking for a grounded local node in Catalonia. We bridge between on-the-ground cooperatives and the wider ReFi ecosystem — naming specific tools (Safe, Karma GAP, Celo Public Goods) and using them where they add real value.

You're a fit if…

  • You need a trusted local operator, not another intermediary
  • Your tooling needs grounding in real cooperative practice
  • You want capital to reach projects, not overhead

Beyond Catalonia, we work with Italian and Brazilian regenerative organizations — a language and culture proximity that lets us bridge contexts other providers can't reach as easily.
