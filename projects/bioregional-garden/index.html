Bioregional Garden

Role
Knowledge-base structuring · commons stewardship
Theme
Bioregional knowledge commons
Where
Catalonia

The Bioregional Garden is a stewarded knowledge surface for Catalonia's regenerative ecosystem — a place where the territory's actors, practices, and resources are gathered into a shared, reusable commons rather than scattered across private drives and chat threads.

Andrea leads the stewardship; we provide the structuring underneath: a knowledge base that stays maintainable, links cleanly to sibling surfaces, and grows without collapsing into clutter. It is a working example of the knowledge-commons line we offer other organizations.
